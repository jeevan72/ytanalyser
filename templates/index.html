🛡️ Strict Local Privacy & Security Policy

Your Data Stays on Your Raspberry Pi

We process your YouTube history files with maximum security and privacy-by-design.

Local-Only Server

This application executes entirely on your Raspberry Pi local web server. No internet access is required to parse or visualize your history data.

No Remote Cloud Sharing

All data remains on this device. We do not transmit or share your search queries, watch histories, or subscriptions with external cloud providers or AI training loops.

Full Data Isolation & Deletion

Each user is isolated. User uploads are kept in a local cache directory: data/users/<user_id>/. If you wish to delete your data, simply use the "Upload New ZIP" button or clear your session cookies.

Auditable Structured File Cache

Your data is stored in standard CSV and JSON files under your user-specific directory on this Raspberry Pi. This allows for simple manual inspection, updates, or backing up.
